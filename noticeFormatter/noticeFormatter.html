MPL Notice Formatter

This tool may be used to format paper notices downloaded from Bibliovation so that they may be easily used with windowed envelopes and perforated paper.

  1. Download (don't print) a PDF of notices from Bibliovation
  2. Highlight all of the text in the PDF from Bibliovation and paste it into the text field below
  3. Click `Format & Print`
  4. Print without headers or footers (available under 'print options' or 'more settings').
