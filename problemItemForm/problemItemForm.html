Problem Item Procedures

Damaged Item or Parts Missing via Counter/Book Drop or Transit Return

  1. Do not check item in!
  2. Add a damaged or parts missing message to patron's checkout screen.
  3. If parts missing, contact patron and retain item for 3 days, then proceed to step 4.
  4. Route item and form to owning library for follow up, or follow local library procedure.

Damaged Item or Parts Missing via Transit Hold

  1. Do not check item in!
  2. Go into item record and select Edit Holds
  3. Change patron's hold priority from "In Transit" to 1 and click Update Hold
  4. Change Custom Status on item screen to Defect Reported.
  5. Route item and form to owning library for follow-up, or follow local library procedure.

Defect Reported

  1. After filling out the above information, check item in.
  2. If a hold is activated, click Ignore.
  3. Change Custom Status to Defect Reported.
  4. Route item with form to owning library, or follow local library procedure.
