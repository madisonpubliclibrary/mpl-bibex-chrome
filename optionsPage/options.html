Separate Hold Shelf Action List

On the Hold Shelf Action List, you can select from the list of item types below to filter those materials into a separate alphabetical list above the standard list of lapsed holds. This is useful for libraries who shelve their CDs or other AV materials separate from their other holds. If activated, OLLs will apear at the top of the hold list, followed by other activated AV filters, followed by the remainder of items.
